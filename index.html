A validated certification is not equal to "lab tested".

Buy third-party tested with confidence

NSF & USP Verified are enforceable standards a product has to meet. Phrases like “lab tested” or “NSF-grade” are just marketing.

Know what's actually checked

A mark can cover contaminant limits, label and dose accuracy, and manufacturing quality, but not every mark covers all three. We show you which.

Know how often it's tested

Some marks verify every production lot; others test periodically. That difference is easy to miss on a label.

Honest about the unknowns

Most products carry no certification at all, but will make many testing claims. Uncertified means unverified, not unsafe. You deserve the difference stated clearly.
